Why is Certification Critical?

Industrial fire suppression systems are responsible for protecting human lives and high-value facilities. Therefore, compliance of the products and systems used with international standards is both a legal requirement and an ethical responsibility.

All products and systems we offer at TATCO have been approved and documented by independent testing organizations. Our customers can be confident that the systems they purchase will operate as expected in a real fire.
