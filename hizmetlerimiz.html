Services

End-to-End Technical Service

Project Design

Fire hazard classification is determined based on your facility's characteristics, and fire suppression system design and calculations are performed in accordance with NFPA 11, EN 13565 and applicable local regulations.

  • Fire hazard class analysis
  • System sizing and selection
  • Technical specifications and drawings

Engineering Services

With our experienced engineering team, fire suppression systems are designed, optimized and technically verified. Flow calculations, pressure analyses and system integration studies are included in the scope.

  • Hydraulic calculations
  • 3D system modeling
  • Technical approval process management

Technical Consulting

Expert independent consulting services are provided on fire safety regulations (NFPA, EN, TSE), insurance requirements and institutional technical standards.

  • Regulatory compliance analysis
  • Risk assessment
  • Insurance reporting support

Commissioning

Factory acceptance test (FAT) and site acceptance test (SAT) processes are managed for installed fire suppression systems, verifying that the system operates according to design parameters.

  • Factory acceptance test (FAT)
  • Site acceptance test (SAT)
  • Operator training

Periodic Maintenance

Annual, semi-annual and monthly maintenance programs are applied to maintain the performance and reliability of your fire suppression systems. All inspections are documented.

  • Scheduled maintenance contracts
  • Component inspection and testing
  • Detailed maintenance reporting

Technical Service

Our 24/7 technical service team is with you throughout Europe for troubleshooting, emergency response, spare parts supply and system revision services.

  • 24/7 emergency response
  • Original spare parts supply
  • System revision and modernization
Our Process

Project Management Process

We manage every project with a systematic and transparent approach.

01
Needs Analysis

Your facility's fire hazard class, structural features and operational conditions are comprehensively assessed.

02
Design & Engineering

System design and technical specification preparation based on the needs analysis are performed.

03
Proposal Submission

A detailed technical and commercial proposal is prepared and presented to the client, with alternatives evaluated.

04
Production & Procurement

Equipment production tracking and procurement process are managed according to the approved project.

05
Installation & Commissioning

The system's site installation is completed with tests and acceptance procedures, then commissioned.

06
Maintenance & Support

Periodic maintenance and 24/7 technical support services continue to be provided for commissioned systems.
