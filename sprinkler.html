Sprinkler Hoses & Accessories

EN 12259 / UL / FM approved, 5 sprinkler head types for different risk classes and applications. K-factor K5.6 to K25; activation temperature 57°C–141°C.

Standard Response Sprinklers

Most widely used head type in commercial, industrial and multi-storey buildings. 5 mm glass bulb, activation 68/79/93/141°C; concealed type 57/74°C. K5.6–K8.0, max. 175 PSI. UL / FM / CE certified.

View Details

Quick Response Sprinklers

QR heads with 3 mm glass bulb (RTI ≤50) providing faster activation than standard. Pendent, upright, concealed and sidewall mounting options. UL / FM / ULc / CE certified.

View Details

Extended Coverage Sprinklers (CMSA)

Extended coverage (CMSA) sprinkler heads providing 20–30 m² protection area per head. High K-factor, fewer pipes and fittings required. NFPA 13 / EN 12259 compliant.

View Details

Storage Type Sprinklers (ESFR)

ESFR heads providing ceiling-only protection for high-rack storage areas without the need for in-rack sprinklers. K14.0–K25 high flow rate; 74°C/96°C activation. UL / FM / ULc / VDS approved.

View Details

Dry Type Sprinklers

Extended-pipe sprinkler specially designed for areas with freezing risk such as cold storage and open spaces. Connected to wet pipe system; extension pipe 100–900 mm, operating range -40°C to +65°C. EN 12259 / UL 199.

View Details
