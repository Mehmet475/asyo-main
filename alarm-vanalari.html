Alarm Valves

UL Listed and FM Approved alarm valves for wet, dry, deluge and pre-action system types. Diameter range 1½″–8″, max. working pressure 250–300 PSI.

Wet Alarm Valve

Controls wet pipe sprinkler systems in environments without freezing risk (above 4°C). When a sprinkler opens, the valve clapper opens; water motor gong and pressure switch activate providing both mechanical and electrical alarm. UL Listed / FM Approved, 3″–8″ diameter, max. 250 PSI.

View Details

Dry Alarm Valve

Designed for areas with freezing risk such as garages, car parks and unheated warehouses. The pipe network is maintained with compressed air or nitrogen; when a sprinkler opens, air escapes and the valve releases water. Accelerator option available, UL/FM approved, max. 300 PSI.

View Details

Deluge Alarm Valve

Designed for areas where fire spreads rapidly, such as flammable material storage and aircraft hangars. All nozzles are open; when the fire detection system is triggered, extinguishing agent is discharged to all points simultaneously. Wet/dry pilot and electric activation. UL Listed / FM Approved, 1½″–8″ diameter.

View Details

Pre-Action Alarm Valve

For areas sensitive to water damage such as archives, galleries and data centers. Piping remains dry; requires independent detection line for activation. Single and double interlock options, UL Listed / FM Approved, 1½″–8″ diameter, max. 250 PSI.
